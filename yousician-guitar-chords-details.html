Build automatic guitar chords generator & enrich chords library

Discover the Yousician Guitar Chords Library on our NextJS website, located at https://yousician.com/guitar-chords/. Explore a wide range of guitar chords and their finger positions with ease. I revamped the chord diagram retrieval system, transforming it into an automatic chord diagram generator. Say goodbye to manual image fetching for each chord - now the system utilizes finger position data to dynamically render the chord diagrams on demand.
